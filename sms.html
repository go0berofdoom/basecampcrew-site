SMS program / opt-in information

BaseCamp family invites

BaseCamp family invites is a transactional SMS program used only to deliver a family invitation requested by an existing BaseCamp user.

One invitation. One message.No recurring messages and no marketing messages are sent through this program.
ProgramBaseCamp family invites
FrequencyVaries; max one message per invite

Supporthello@basecampcrew.com

How opt-in works

A transactional SMS is sent only when an existing BaseCamp user explicitly invites a family member by entering that family member’s phone number in the BaseCamp app and confirming that they have that person’s consent to receive the invitation.

The invited person does not enroll in a recurring messaging program. Message frequency varies, with a maximum of one message per invite. No recurring or marketing messages are sent.

Example message

BASECAMP FAMILY INVITES“Alex invited you to join their family on BaseCamp. Open your invitation: [link]. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.”

Required disclosures

  • Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Message frequency varies (max: per-invite).
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Consent and privacy

Consent to receive this invitation is not a condition of purchase. An existing BaseCamp user must confirm they have the family member’s consent before submitting the number. The number is used only to deliver the requested one-time invite and support required opt-out handling.

See the BaseCamp Privacy Policy for more information.

Support

For help with BaseCamp family invites, reply HELP or email hello@basecampcrew.com.
